POKEMON GEN 1

PIKACHU

Pikachu is an iconic Electric-type Pokémon,
widely recognized as the mascot of the entire Pokémon
franchise.
Known for its yellow fur, lightning bolt-shaped tail, and red,
electricity-storing cheek sacs,
it first appeared in Pokémon Red and Blue and became world-famous as Ash Ketchum's partner in the anime series
